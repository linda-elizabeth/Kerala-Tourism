Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta, the smallest district in God’s own country is renowned as the centre of pilgrim Worship in Kerala. The famous shrine of Lord Ayyappa in Sabari hills is situated in Pathanamthitta. Every year crores of pilgrims visit the adobe of Ayyappa in the sacred sabari hills. Pathanamthitta is also known for learning and experiencing of many cultural and traditional practices of Kerala. This is a land of cultural training centres, water fiestas and pilgrim centres.


Sabarimala

Sabarimala Sree Dharmasastha Temple,dedicated to Lord Ayappa is one of the most famous pilgrim centre in Kerala.This temple is situated on the sacred Sabari Hills about 914 metres above sea level. The temple is amidst the thick forests in the refrains of the Western Ghats.

Aranmula

Aranmula, a heritage village situated on the banks of the famous river Pamba is famous for diverse attractions. During Onam a Snake Boat Race is held here, at the stretch of river Pampa as part of the festival during the period August / September.

Parumala

Parumala is a famous Christian Pilgrim centre in Pathanamthitta district. This is India’s first church. The church houses the tomb of St Geevarghese Mar Gregorios, Malankara church’s first saint. The remembrance day celebrated during November every year draws thousands of people from different part of the state.
