Kollam

Kollam (erstwhile Quilon) is a southern district of Kerala, located 70 km north of the state’s capital Thiruvananthapuram.. Kollam is the capital of Kerala's cashew industry. Plains, mountains, lakes, lagoons, and backwaters, forests, farmland and rivers make up the topography of the district. The area had trading relationships with Phoenicia and Ancient Rome.


Thangassery

Thangassery is a historically important place situated 5 km away from Kollam town. The main attraction of this place is the lighthouse. This light house is 144 ft. tall and stands as a sentinel, warning seamen of the treacherous reefs of Thangassery.

Jatayu Earth Centre

Jatayu Earth Center, also known as Jatayu Nature Park or Jatayu Rock, is a park and tourism centre at Chadayamangalam in Kollam district of Kerala.

Palaruvi Waterfalls

A stunningly beautiful waterfall in Kollam, surrounded by ruins of ancient temples. The word Palaruvi means ‘Stream of Milk’.This waterfall is unique.The water in Palaruvi is said to be medicated and is preventive against many diseases.
